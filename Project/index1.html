Brownie recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 box chocolate

  • 1 1/4cup water
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup heavy whipped cream
  • 1 bag semi-sweet chocolate morsels

Directions

  1. preheat oven to 300 degrees
  2. prepare baking dish
  3. melt butter
  4. mix in sweetener and salt
  5. mix in wet ingredients
  6. mix in dry ingredients
  7. add batter to pan
  8. cool before cutting (if youre patient)
